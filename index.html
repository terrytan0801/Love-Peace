Common Inquiries

Booking FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions about band setup, custom playlists, equipment, and booking details.

How far in advance should we book Love Peace?

We typically recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for weekend dates during peak wedding and corporate seasons. However, feel free to contact us for last-minute inquiries as we may have availability or associate performers.

Can we customize the band's playlist or request specific songs?

Yes, absolutely! We provide a comprehensive repertoire list covering pop, jazz, classics, and bilingual hits. Additionally, we are happy to prepare up to 3 custom song requests (such as your march-in or first dance) that are not on our standard repertoire list.

Do you provide the sound system (PA) and stage equipment?

Yes, we provide state-of-the-art sound systems (high-quality active speakers, mixing console, premium wireless mics, and instruments). For smaller venues, our standard PA is included. For larger ballrooms (over 30 tables) or outdoor venues, we offer customized sound system scaleups with professional sound engineers to ensure crystal-clear acoustics.

Can the band perform bilingual or multilingual songs?

Yes, our vocalists are highly versatile and sing fluently in English, Mandarin, Cantonese, and select regional dialects. This is perfect for multicultural weddings and multinational corporate galas where a broad musical language is appreciated.

What is the duration of your performance sets?

Our standard live band packages include 2 sets of 45 minutes each, spread over a 2 to 3-hour banquet schedule. Emcee packages cover the entire formal segment of your event, coordinating key milestones (speeches, toast, cake cutting) and games. Set timings can be dynamically aligned with your actual banquet flow.
